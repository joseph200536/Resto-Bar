Dishes

Samosa

A samosa is a popular triangular-shaped savory pastry originating from the Indian subcontinent. It typically consists of a crispy outer layer made from dough, filled with a mixture of spiced potatoes, peas, lentils, or minced meat.

Noodles

Noodles are a versatile staple food enjoyed worldwide, originating from various cultures such as China, Italy, and Japan. They come in a variety of shapes, sizes, and flavors, made from wheat, rice, buckwheat, or other grains

Egg

Eggs are a nutritious and versatile food enjoyed worldwide, known for their high protein content and versatility in cooking. They can be prepared in numerous ways, including scrambled, fried, boiled, poached, or baked.

Salad

Salad refers to a dish composed of a mixture of raw or cooked vegetables, fruits, grains, proteins, and dressings. It is often served chilled and can be customized according to personal preferences and dietary needs.

Pizza

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish consisting of a round, flattened dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various ingredients such as meats, vegetables, and herbs. It is typically baked in an oven, resulting in a crispy crust and melted cheese

Chicken

Chicken is one of the most widely consumed meats globally, prized for its versatility, mild flavor, and relatively low fat content. It can be prepared in various ways, including grilled, roasted, fried, baked, or simmered in soups and stews.
